# Onea Pricing Research and Commercial Model 2026

Reviewed: 15 June 2026

## Positioning decision

Onea is not priced as a product reseller or a single-discipline freelancer. The company profile describes a Level 1 B-BBEE, VAT-registered, CSD-listed partner delivering technology, connectivity, digital marketing, communications, production, media buying, cloud, ERP and procurement support.

The public price is therefore a maximum realistic market anchor. The protected floor supports negotiation without forcing sales to increase a price after a client has configured it.

Each near-final estimate includes:

1. Supplier product or platform allowance where applicable.
2. Onea discovery and solution design.
3. Delivery labour from the relevant internal disciplines.
4. Project, procurement and account administration.
5. Testing, documentation and handover.
6. Risk and revision capacity.
7. Sustainable profit and a protected negotiation band.

## Market evidence

### Managed hosting

- xneelo publicly advertises raw web hosting from R99/month.
- Source: https://xneelo.co.za/
- Xneelo infrastructure and domain products use the published VAT-inclusive supplier price with a fixed 20% Onea markup. Registration and renewal prices are maintained separately by extension; unpublished transfer charges require confirmation.

### Microsoft 365

- Microsoft lists Business Basic at USD 6, Business Standard at USD 12.50 and Business Premium at USD 22 per user/month on annual billing, excluding VAT.
- Source: https://www.microsoft.com/en-za/microsoft-365/business/microsoft-365-plans-and-pricing
- The R349 Business Standard allowance includes currency and channel movement plus Onea billing and administration. The exact CSP feed can replace the allowance without changing the service structure.

### Digital marketing

- South African full digital packages are benchmarked at roughly R21,000-R29,000/month, averaging about R25,000.
- Source: https://www.procompare.co.za/prices/digital-marketing-seo-services/digital-marketing-packages
- A recent South African agency comparison places specialist agency retainers around R25,000-R55,000/month depending on scope.
- Source: https://circlemedia.co.za/agency-vs-in-house-the-true-cost-of-digital-marketing-in-south-africa/
- Onea's anchors are R7,500 Starter, R14,500 Growth and R28,500 Full Agency.

### Google Ads management

- Google allows advertisers to control and change their own daily budgets. Media spend is therefore kept separate from Onea's management fee.
- Source: https://business.google.com/en-all/google-ads/campaign-budget/
- Published South African management packages range from roughly R2,600 to R7,800/month, while broader South African comparison pricing places Google Ads services around R3,800-R7,500/month.
- Sources: https://www.bunnypants.co.za/google-ads-management-services-south-africa-price-packages/ and https://www.procompare.co.za/prices/digital-marketing-seo-services/google-ads-marketing
- Global agency pricing commonly uses 10%-20% of media spend, usually with a minimum monthly fee.
- Source: https://clicksgeek.com/google-ads-management-pricing/
- Onea uses a predictable certified-service model: R6,500 once-off setup, then R4,500 Starter, R7,500 Growth or R12,000 Scale management per month. Advertising spend is paid separately to Google. Performance Max, Shopping, offline conversion tracking, landing pages and creative support are separately selectable.

### Long-distance field travel

- The SARS prescribed reimbursive travel rate is R4.95 per kilometre from March 1, 2026.
- Source: https://www.sars.gov.za/guide-for-employers-in-respect-of-allowances/
- Onea's 50-100km call-out remains R2,250. For destinations more than 100km from the Pretoria operations base, the website calculates a live driving route. The R2,250 base covers the first 100km one way; excess distance is charged as a return trip at R4.95/km and rounded up to the nearest R10.
- Accommodation is excluded from the automated travel amount because an overnight requirement depends on scheduling and project duration.

### Website and platform development

- Established South African web rates commonly range from R450-R950/hour, with small business projects starting around R5,590 and custom builds reaching R60,000 or more.
- Source: https://www.newperspectivestudio.co.za/wp/what-is-the-cost-of-creating-a-website-in-south-africa/
- Onea's agreed Basic entry website starts at R6,500 for four simple business pages. Each additional page and functional upgrade is priced separately, with e-commerce at R45,000 and a backend or portal foundation at R65,000.

### Creative and brand

- South African corporate identity packages publicly list logo work around R6,800 and a standard identity around R9,500 before VAT.
- Source: https://www.elevationdesign.co.za/logo-design-branding/
- Onea's R12,500 base includes strategy and creative direction, while the R25,000 identity option supports a fuller corporate system.

### Video production

- Pretoria/Johannesburg-facing production packages are publicly listed at R8,700, R13,500 and R22,800.
- Source: https://tcproductions.co.za/video-production-packages/
- Onea uses those real package anchors and adds explicit photography, drone and additional-edit options.

### CCTV and installed infrastructure

- South African CCTV installation labour is commonly quoted around R2,000-R5,000 before equipment.
- Source: https://www.cctvinstaller-gauteng.co.za/camera-installation-price/
- Onea's R7,500 base includes assessment, equipment planning and installation setup. Camera, recorder and access-control quantities then change the estimate directly.

### ERP and cloud

- A current South African ERP guide places implementation setup broadly around R100,000-R500,000 depending on scope.
- Source: https://vasyerp.com/en-za/blog/erp-implementation-for-south-africa/
- Onea's R150,000 ERP implementation foundation is an upper-SME starting anchor, with discovery, cloud foundation, migration and monthly administration priced separately.
- Azure consumption remains usage-based and should be calculated with Microsoft's calculator.
- Source: https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/pricing/calculator/

### Media buying

- Agency media fees commonly run from about 3%-15% of placement spend.
- Source: https://elementthree.com/guide-to-marketing-agencies/agency-pricing-and-costs/
- Onea uses predictable management bands: R7,500/month up to R50,000 placement spend, R15,000/month for R50,000-R150,000, and R25,000/month above R150,000. Placement spend is excluded.

## Hardware margin policy

Hardware is no longer treated as a thin pass-through:

| Category | Display markup | Floor markup |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| Apple | 35% | 15% |
| General hardware | 45% | 20% |
| Networking | 50% | 22% |
| CCTV | 50% | 22% |
| UPS and power | 45% | 20% |
| POS | 45% | 20% |
| Licensing | 30% | 12% |

The procurement, setup, deployment, migration and management services remain separate line items. This prevents the product margin from carrying the full delivery burden.

## Estimate confidence

The public builder shows an expected final adjustment range:

- Service rate cards: normally within 5%.
- Media production, ERP and cloud: normally within 8%.
- Installed WiFi, CCTV and networking: normally within 8%-10%.
- General supplier allowances: normally within 12%.

The tolerance assumes accurate answers and unchanged scope. Supplier substitution, media placement spend, exchange-rate movement and undisclosed site conditions remain valid reasons for movement.

## Internal delivery structure

The protected catalogue maps each builder to the disciplines required from Onea's company profile:

- Executive and commercial oversight
- Operations and project management
- Client and campaign strategy
- Technology architecture
- Senior development and integration
- Cloud and IT infrastructure
- Network engineering and installation
- ERP and systems support
- Communications and PR
- Creative direction and design
- Office, procurement and accounts administration

The stored floor rates are delivery economics, not employee salary information.

## Review rule

Review service rates quarterly. Review public market anchors monthly. Refresh supplier-fed products at least every 30 minutes when production credentials and feeds are available. Any item without a current feed can still use an approved market anchor, but its tolerance must be higher and the source must be recorded.
